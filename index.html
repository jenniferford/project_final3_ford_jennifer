About

Harryhausen's is Monstropolis' best restaurant for date night or any night, really. Our chefs are dedicated to quality work hard to bring you the best Japanese inspired dishes. Come taste our world class cuisine. You might even spot a child or two while your here.

Meet Our Chef


Our sushi chefs are highly trained experts dedicated to bringing you high quality food, with class.

Hours

Harryhausen's is open Tuesday through Saturday. We open at 3pm and close when the chefs say so.

Location

Harryhausen's is located right in the heart on downtown Monstropolis at 1234 Poe Way.
Please give us a call at (221) 345-8765 or email us at reservations@harryhausens.com to make a reservation. Reservations are highly recommended and by highly recommended, we mean you won't get in without one. Valet parking is available.
